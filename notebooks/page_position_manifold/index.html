Page Position Manifold
Layer 0 / 0
Each point is the mean residual-stream activation for tokens at a specific position on the “page” — a (line #, # of characters into the line) tuple. We average these activations across a corpus of 5000 book passages which have linebreaks every 150 characters. We project mean activations into PCA space. Color encodes position: hue encodes horizontal position (blue at left margin, red at right), lightness encodes line number (light = early lines, dark = later lines).
